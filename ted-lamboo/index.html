01 — What We Discussed

The accounting workflow, mapped

We walked through how data moves through your firm today — from raw documents to client-ready outputs — and identified where AI shifts you from doing the work to auditing the work.

📥
Intake
Where data enters
Invoices, receipts, tax documents, client emails, bank statements — arriving via email, mail, portal, and phone. Currently requires manual sorting, entry, and filing.
⚙
Processing
Where effort lives
Data entry into QuickBooks or Sage. GL coding, bank reconciliation, payroll calculation, GST/PST tracking. Repetitive, high-volume, error-prone when done manually.
📊
Reporting
Where value is delivered
Financial statements, tax returns, client advisories, year-end packages. The work your clients see — and the work that earns trust and retains relationships.
