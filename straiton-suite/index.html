About the Suite

Warm, considered, and built to actually live in.

This is an above-ground one-bedroom that's easy to mistake for a standalone cottage. The private French-door entry opens to a living room anchored by a stone gas fireplace with a reclaimed wood mantle — the kind of space that earns its warmth.

The kitchen is unusually generous for a suite this size: live-edge wood counters on one side, a quartz-topped island on the other, and a full stainless appliance package including a real dishwasher. Storage is built in — double closets in the bedroom, a linen tower in the bathroom, and a separate pantry room off the kitchen.

In-suite stacked laundry means no shared machines. A walk-in glass shower keeps the bathroom feeling modern rather than improvised. Hydro, water, and heat are included — tenant only arranges internet.
